Changing Sentences

  • Identify the tense first, then change auxiliary verbs as needed.
  • Affirmative → Negative: add not or use negative forms.
  • Assertive → Interrogative: move auxiliary before the subject.
  • Keep subject–verb agreement correct.

Example:
He is honest → Is he honest?
She was dancing → Was she dancing?

Tag Questions

  • Use the helping verb + subject pronoun as the tag.
  • Positive sentence → negative tag; Negative sentence → positive tag.

Example:
He is a student, isn’t he?
They don’t play football, do they?
